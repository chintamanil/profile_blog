About Us

EasyPaint is changing the way people view the world. With color.

Through our innovative painting experience, homeowners are connected to our certified paint crews without wasting any time or money. EasyPaint brings style and a personal touch into their homes with just a few clicks. Perfection guaranteed.

  1. Marty Cornish

    CEO

    In school when Marty looked at the sandwich his mother packed him for lunch he didn’t just see turkey on rye. He saw an opportunity. After selling his lunches throughout school, Marty continued his entrepreneurial spirit by creating his first company, Round Trip Sports. While also working as a store manager at Sherwin-Williams Paints, he worked with Oliver, who shared a passion for efficiency and over-the-top customer service. At just 27, Marty sold Round Trip Sports to focus all of his energy on building EasyPaint. Which is good news as he no longer has to sell his lunches.

  2. Oliver Harriehausen

    COO

    Oliver’s passion and hard work ethic is a daily inspiration to the entire EasyPaint team. Previously, Oliver worked in the paint industry as a Sales Representative for Sherwin-Williams Paints, where he saw first hand the need for a simpler process. There he met Marty, who not only was also able to hold his own speaking Spanish, but shared the goal of running a business someday. When not tackling the role as EasyPaint's COO, Oliver is keeping track of his finger paint crew, sons Matias and Noah.

  3. Denis Abrams

    EVP of Business Development

    South African born Denis brings his expertise in the painting industry from his role as CEO of industry-leader Benjamin-Moore Paints. Working directly under Warren Buffett, he proved his business savvy by becoming the first CEO to sell paint online. An avid distance runner, Denis comes to EasyPaint with a passion for technology and two adorable granddaughters.
